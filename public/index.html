Our Framework

A repeatable investigation in five steps

Every Safehub investigation follows the same structured path — so every report meets the same standard, regardless of who initiates it.

01

Capture

Ingest incident notes, witness statements, photos, equipment logs and site context — typed, spoken or uploaded.

02

Contextualize

Cross-reference your site register, prior incidents, asset history and active controls to ground the investigation.

03

Analyze

Apply the right methodology — 5 Whys, ICAM, Fishbone or Bow-Tie — and surface root, contributing and systemic causes.

04

Recommend

Generate ranked corrective actions mapped to the Hierarchy of Controls, with owners and target dates.

05

Document

Produce a regulator-ready report with full evidence chain, audit trail, and exportable formats.

Resources

The methodologies Safehub uses

We don't invent a new investigation standard. We apply the ones HSE teams already trust — consistently and at speed.

5 Whys

Iterative questioning to drill from symptom to underlying root cause.

ICAM

Incident Cause Analysis Method — separates absent/failed defences from individual and organisational factors.

Fishbone (Ishikawa)

Categorical cause-and-effect mapping across people, process, equipment and environment.

Bow-Tie

Visualises threats, top events, consequences, and the preventive and mitigative barriers between them.

Hierarchy of Controls

Ranks corrective actions from elimination through to PPE so the strongest controls come first.

WHS Act alignment

Outputs structured to support due-diligence obligations and regulator-facing disclosures.
