Our Framework

A repeatable investigation in five steps

Every Safehub investigation follows the same structured path. Every report meets the same standard, regardless of who initiates it.

01

Capture

Ingest incident notes, witness statements, photos, equipment logs and site context. Typed, spoken or uploaded.

02

Contextualize

Cross-reference your site register, prior incidents, asset history and active controls to ground the investigation.

03

Analyse

Apply ICAM and 5 Whys to surface contributing factors, systemic gaps, and absent defences.

04

Recommend

Generate suggested corrective actions mapped to the Hierarchy of Controls, with owners and target dates. Always flagged as suggested pending site review.

05

Document

Produce a regulator-ready report with full evidence chain, audit trail, and exportable formats.

Resources

The methodologies Safehub uses

We don't invent a new investigation standard. We apply the ones HSE teams already trust. Applied consistently and at speed.

5 Whys

Iterative questioning to drill from the immediate event down to systemic contributing factors.

ICAM

Incident Cause Analysis Method. Separates absent/failed defences from individual and organisational factors.

Hierarchy of Controls

Ranks corrective actions from elimination through to PPE so the strongest controls come first.

ISO 45001 alignment

Findings and recommended controls structured to align with ISO 45001 occupational health and safety principles.

Information gap tagging

Every finding tagged as FACT, INFERENCE, or PATTERN. Missing facts surfaced as explicit gaps for the investigator.

Jurisdiction awareness

Regulatory references and notification triggers matched to AU, NZ, UK, EU, CA, or US legislation.
